UBI-ID System: A Next-Generation Economic Framework

Author: Enes Balıkçı  |  France, 2025

Abstract

The UBI-ID System is a hybrid socio-economic infrastructure designed to distribute Universal Basic Income (UBI) fairly, based on three verifiable parameters: formal education, civic contribution, and legal behavior. This system integrates government-verified digital identities and official certifications to ensure security, scalability, and justice in wealth distribution.
